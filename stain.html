Stain

Wood stain services for a natural, protected finish.

Stain work brings out the character of wood while adding a protective finish for decks, trim, doors, railings, and wood accents.

Careful wood preparation

Stain depends on the condition of the wood. We clean, sand, and prepare the surface before applying stain for an even appearance.

Options can include transparent, semi-transparent, or solid color stain depending on the surface and desired look.

  • Deck and porch staining
  • Doors, railings, and trim
  • Sanding and surface prep
  • Protective finish options

Stain detail

Wood finishes need the right prep and product choice.

Surface condition

Wood is checked for old coating, wear, dirt, and sanding needs before stain is applied.

Finish selection

Transparent, semi-transparent, and solid stain options can change the final look and protection level.

Exterior protection

Decks, railings, and exterior wood are finished with exposure and maintenance in mind.
