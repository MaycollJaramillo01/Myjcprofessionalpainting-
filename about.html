About the company

Founded in 2018 and built on 16 years of painting experience.

Myjcprofessionalpainting brings practical jobsite experience, careful preparation, and respectful service to every project.

Our standard

We believe great painting starts before the first coat.

Clean finishes depend on preparation. That means protecting the space, repairing rough areas, sanding, caulking, priming correctly, and applying the right paint for the surface.

Every project is handled with direct communication and a focus on results that look professional when the tools are packed away.

Careful preparation

Surfaces are reviewed and prepared before paint is applied.

Respect for your space

Floors, furniture, fixtures, and surrounding areas are protected.

Clear contact

One phone number and one email make scheduling simple.

How we work

Built around preparation, respect, and clear communication.

Before the work

We review the surfaces, discuss the scope, identify repairs, and confirm the best preparation for the finish you want.

During the project

We keep the work area organized, protect nearby spaces, and handle each coat with attention to coverage and detail.

After completion

We inspect the finished work, review touch-ups, and leave the space ready for normal use.
